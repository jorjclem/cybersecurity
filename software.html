Harmful softwares

Harmful softwares are called malware. These can be described as viruses, ransomware, botnet, worm, spyware, and so much more. Ransomware is used for the main purpose of getting money out of people. While botnet, will infect one computer in a network and spread to the others. Adware plans a virus through pop-up ads, there are so many forms of malware its important to stay vigilant when downloading things on your computer. To prevent these we use anti-virus softwares. This can include firewalls. This can block malware as long as they are enabled and updated.
